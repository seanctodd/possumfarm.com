Hello. I am Papi Possum.

You found my website. This is either a sign of great intelligence on your part, or you got here by accident. Either way, **welcome**. The possums and I are glad you are here.

I started this farm in 2003. At the time, people told me I was crazy. They said, "Papi, you can't just start a possum farm." They said, "Papi, that's not a real business." They said, "Papi, put the possum down, you're at a Wendy's."

They were all wrong. Except maybe that last one.

## Why Possums?

Great question. The possum chose me, not the other way around.

It was a Tuesday. I was sitting on my porch eating a sandwich when a possum climbed up onto the railing and stared at me. Not in a threatening way. Just... *knowingly*. Like she understood something about the universe that I didn't.

I gave her half my sandwich. She ate it, hissed once out of habit, and then lay down next to me and went to sleep.

I thought: *this is the animal I want to spend my life with.*

**Her name was Bernadette.** She is still alive. She is 21 years old. Possums don't usually live that long but Bernadette has never been informed of this and I'm not going to be the one to tell her.

## What We Do Here

At Possum Farm, we do three things:

1. **We raise possums** — organically, ethically, and with great enthusiasm
2. **We educate people** — about how possums are actually incredible and you should stop being scared of them
3. **We sell merchandise** — because the farm doesn't run on love alone (though Papi's love for possums is considerable)

We do NOT:
- Eat possums (this is extremely important to Papi, please do not ask)
- Race possums (they refuse and I respect their boundaries)
- Dress possums in tiny hats (they allow this but Papi feels it's undignified)

## The Future

I have big plans for Possum Farm. Soon we will have:

- **Possum Cam** — a 24/7 livestream of the possums doing possum things (mostly sleeping)
- **Possum CSA** — a community-supported agriculture box, but instead of vegetables it's just possum facts and a picture of a possum
- **The Papi Possum Podcast** — conversations with possums, translated into English by Papi (he speaks some possum, he says)

Thank you for visiting. Please tell your friends about the possums. They deserve the recognition.

With love and opposable thumbs (the possums have these, I'm fine with regular thumbs),

**Papi Possum**
