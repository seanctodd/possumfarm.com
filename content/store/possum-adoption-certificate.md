Have you ever wanted a possum of your own but lack the 40 acres, the emotional fortitude, and the specific lifestyle that possum ownership requires?

We have the solution.

**Adopt-A-Possum** is Possum Farm's symbolic adoption program. For a one-time fee, you receive:

### What You Get

**Immediate delivery:**
- A beautiful, officially certified Possum Farm Adoption Certificate with your name and your possum's name in fancy lettering
- Your possum's official biography (a full-page portrait of their personality, habits, and opinions)
- A 5x7 photograph of your possum doing possum things

**Ongoing (digital, monthly):**
- A monthly possum update from Papi — how your possum is doing, what they've been eating, any new developments in their possum journey
- Access to the Possum Cam when we get it working (it's almost working, Gerald is on it)

### The Possums Available for Adoption

- **Rodrigo** — Large, dramatic. His hobbies include knocking things over and reconsidering.
- **Linda** — Small, fast, has never been fully explained. Scientists are baffled. Papi is not.
- **The Colonel** — Named for his bearing, not any military service. Walks with great purpose. Sleeps 22 hours a day.
- **Spooky** — Looks intimidating, is actually very gentle. Named during a phase.
- **Gerald Jr.** — Yes, named after Gerald. Gerald named him. It was a whole thing.

*Note: Symbolic adoption does not grant physical custody of the possum. The possum lives at the farm. The possum does not know you exist and that is completely fine. The possum is living its best life and you are supporting that. This is beautiful.*
