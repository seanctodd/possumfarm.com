Meet **Bernadette**. She is 21 years old. She is the world's oldest possum. She has seen things you cannot comprehend. And now she is on your mug, watching you drink your morning coffee with the same knowing expression she gave Papi back in 2003.

This ceramic mug features Bernadette's official portrait — a hand-drawn illustration commissioned from a local artist who described the experience as "challenging but deeply meaningful."

**On one side:** Bernadette in her glory, with the caption *"BERNADETTE, EST. 2003"*
**On the other side:** Her personal wisdom: *"Do less. Hiss more. Rest always."*

Microwave safe. Dishwasher safe. Possum safe (Bernadette approved it before we printed it; it took three sessions because she kept falling asleep).
